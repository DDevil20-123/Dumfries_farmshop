Our farm petting zoo's anminals are very tame and accustomed to human interaction, offering visitors a chance to learn about farm life while engaging with the animals.
below you can see the different types of anminals we have with us:
Goats - Friendly and curious, goats are one of our most popular animals. Kids can feed and pet them.
Sheep - Soft and fluffy, sheep are also gentle animals that enjoy interaction.
Pigs - these are fun to pet, especially when their younger.
Chickens - Friendly chickens can be seen roaming or in coops. Visitors can learn about egg-laying, and can feed them seeds, which are supplied onsite.
Rabbits - Small and cuddly, rabbits are a favorite for young visitors who can enjoy petting and holding them.
Cows - can be seen grazing in the fields around the petting zoo area, and while large the are gentle giants and very approchable.
Ponies - These are great for small rides or simply for petting.
Ducks - fun to feed, particularly by the pond or water features, where they can be found through the year.
