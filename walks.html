We offer some forest walks, with donation stands along some points along our paths,
and are opertunities for visitors to help support the local community and charitys in the area.
One of our walks takes you past a hidden pond, a favourite spot for our ducks,
that often take shelter in the reeds when its gets windy, which does make them harder to spot,
but will always make an effort to come out when encouraged by the promise of food.
So we kindly provide seeds that can be found at the donation stands if you wish to feed the ducks.

One of our more popular walks comes into its own as autumn comes into full swing.
As all of the leaves start to turn, deep red, and brown, and there is a nice crunch under foot.
With a peacful river flowing alongside, with occationaly a duck or two.
